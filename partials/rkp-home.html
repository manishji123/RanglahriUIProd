Why another astrology site?

For precise & accurate chart reading, I felt the need of a software which can provide :
  • Holistic horoscope view
  • Reduce manual calculation for widely used parameters
  • Placidous house system
  • KP based calculation with traditional charts
  • KP Horary chart (Horay Number : 1-249)
  • Additional features/calculations
Note 
  • Retrogade planet is represented by "-R" suffix with planet abbreviation e.g. retrogade mercury is Me-R .
  • Calculation of Untenanted & Self-starred plantes. represented by s, u e.g.
    • Mes ⇒ Self starred Mercury
    • Meu ⇒ UnTenanted Mercury
    • Mes,u ⇒ Self starred & UnTenanted Mercury
Features 
  • Geo:
    • Broad geo atlas of entire world with the manual entry options
    • Ability to capture local Longitude, Latitude & timezone...
    • Automatic DST, timezone calculation
  • Rotation:
    • Rotate chart... rotate everythig.. adjust the cusps/planets/destiny matrix tables as well
    • Individual divisional (Varga) charts Rotation
  • Visual 360° chart depicting cusp length/Rasi overlap and exact location of planet on 360° horizon.
  • Cusps Table:
    • Full details of each cusp:
      • All Rasi/Nakshatra with their % length occupied in cusp
      • Sublord, Sub-sublord & corresponding Badhak cusp
  • Planets Table:
    • NakshatraLord [NL], SubLord [SL] & Sub-subLord [SS]
    • Cusp point [CP]: Exact position in cusp (value : 0-1) e.g. 0 ⇒ start of cusp, 0.5 ⇒ middle of cusp, 1 ⇒ end of cusp
    • Primary owned cusp by RasiLord [R], NakshatraLord [N], SubLord[S] &  Sub-subLord [SS]
      • Primary ⇒ Rising
    • Secondary owned cusp by RasiLord [R] & NakshatraLord [N]
      • Seconday ⇒ Non-rising
    • Badhak cusp ⇒ Planets are badhak to the cusp based on occupied cusp[C], owned-rasi[R], nakshatra[N] & sublord[S]
  • Detailed Destiny matrix:
    • Occupied cusp[C], Lord of Rasis [R], Lord of Nakshatra [N], Lord of SubLord [S], Lord of Sub-subLord [SS]
    • Planet[PL], NakshatraLord[NL], SubLord[SL], NakshatraLord of SubLord [SN]
  • Dasa:
    • MahaDasa, AntarDasa, Pratyantar & Sookshma
    • Transitory (Gochar) position at the time of Dasa start at all level
    • Detailed transit (Gochar) calculation based on horoscope
